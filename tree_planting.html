Tree Planting

Tree planting is the act of growing trees to improve the environment, support ecosystems, and combat climate change.

Importance: Trees help clean the air, prevent soil erosion, and provide habitats for animals.

Benefits: Tree planting leads to cooler temperatures, better oxygen production, and enhanced biodiversity.

Actions: We can plant trees by participating in tree-planting campaigns, maintaining existing trees, and encouraging others to plant.
