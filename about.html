ABOUT US














Welcome to our Climate Change Awareness project! We are a group of IT students from CHMSU – Carlos Hilado Memorial State University, dedicated to promoting environmental knowledge and inspiring positive action.

Why This Project Matters

Climate change affects people around the world. Even a temperature rise of 1.5°C can lead to stronger storms, floods, and loss of natural habitats. Our purpose is to share knowledge—because even small actions can create big positive impacts.

Our Goals

  • Educate our community about climate change
  • Encourage sustainable choices in daily life
  • Promote awareness on reducing harmful gases like CO2 and CH4
  • Support environmental activities and youth initiatives
