Climate Change Awareness
















What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be natural, but human activities — especially burning fossil fuels — are now the main cause.

Causes of Climate Change

  • Burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas)
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial emissions such as CO2
  • Agriculture and livestock producing CH4 (methane)

Effects of Climate Change

  • Rising global temperature by about 1.2°C since 1880
  • Melting ice caps and glaciers
  • Stronger storms, floods, and droughts
  • Rising sea levels
  • Loss of wildlife habitats

How We Can Help

  1. Save energy at home
  2. Use public transport or walk
  3. Reduce, reuse, recycle
  4. Plant trees
  5. Support renewable energy sources

Conclusion

Climate change is a global issue, but even small actions from each person can make a big difference. Together, we can protect our planet for future generations.
