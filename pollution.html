Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment, which affects air, water, and soil quality and human and animal health.

Causes: Pollution is caused by industrial waste, vehicle emissions, plastic disposal, and chemical use in agriculture.

Effects: It leads to respiratory diseases, water contamination, climate change, loss of biodiversity, and global warming.

Solutions: Reduce pollution by using clean energy, recycling waste, reducing vehicle emissions, and raising environmental awareness.
